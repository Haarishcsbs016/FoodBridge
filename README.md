🍱 FeedForward – Food Donation Platform

FeedForward is a MERN stack web application that connects restaurants with surplus food to NGOs and food banks, enabling quick food donations and pickup before the food expires.

The platform helps reduce food waste and fight hunger by creating a digital bridge between food donors and organizations that distribute food to communities in need.

🌍 Problem Statement

Large amounts of edible food are wasted daily by restaurants, events, and food outlets. At the same time, many NGOs and food banks struggle to provide food to people in need.

There is no centralized digital system that efficiently connects food donors with organizations that can collect and distribute surplus food.

FeedForward solves this problem by providing a real-time food donation platform.

🚀 Features
👤 User Authentication

Secure registration and login

JWT authentication

Password encryption using bcrypt

🍽 Food Donation Listing

Restaurants can post surplus food details

Includes quantity, pickup time, and expiry time

🏥 NGO Food Claiming

NGOs can browse available food listings

NGOs can claim food donations

🔔 Pickup Notification System

NGOs notify restaurants when they arrive for pickup

Restaurants receive pickup alerts

⏱ Expiry Monitoring

Food listings automatically expire after the expiry time

📊 Donation Tracking

Restaurants can track their donations

NGOs can track claimed pickups
